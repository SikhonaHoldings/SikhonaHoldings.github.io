frequently asked questions

FAQs

Do you charge delivery?

Outside of a 20km radius we are forced to charge delivery as the TLB cannot be driven for long distances.

What is the minimum hours I can hire the TLB for?

Though we charge by the hour,we work on a minimum of 4 hours.

How many hours is half day and how many hours is full day?

Half day is 4 hours and Full day is 8 hours.

If your minimum call out is 4 hours and I require only 2,am I able to pay the 4 hour rate for call out to be possible?

Yes you can call us out and any remaining balance on your hours can be used however you wish.We can dig a pit,clear your yard or even do your neighbour's work to deplete the remaining hours.A refund is not possible on the remaining hours.

What is a dry rate and what is a wet rate?
A dry rate is when the TLB is hired alone and the diesel is at the customer's expense
A wet rate is when the client hires the TLB with diesel.

For households we charge a wet rate and for contracts we charge a dry rate.

Do you accept cash? or Do I pay upfront?

We higly discourage this method of payment but on special conditions allow it
Payment is strictly made upfront.

Do you Nomfundo Ngcobo come on site to ensure service delivery?

Sikhona Holdings employs a trusted team of Operators who are fully competetent who's work can be fully trusted however our operations officer is always on site to oversee the work
In exceptional instances and upon request I can avail myself.
